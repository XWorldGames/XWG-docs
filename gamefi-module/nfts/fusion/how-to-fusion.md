# How-To Fusion

**Fusing your Dream Cards:**

Access the Dream Card Evolution page by going to https://xwg.games, clicking “Fusion” on our navigation bar at the top, and then selecting “Card Fusion”.

You will then be shown the following menu:

1. Log in to the "Fusion" button on the navigation bar of the X World Games website to enter the "Card Fusion" page.

![](https://lh5.googleusercontent.com/uMymn2r-KtUyujxjgMwPjhs7TXk1GWfmigeZJnpVc76TZq6PG0sR-YI2zAKbE\_ZtR52BiRa7WOsozmvJr7ZxkJpQrTdvP-4FlUCL1P2JFCBifeL10auqgn8Yi6Nk8oh2TRZwskJW3CZuAvP3U9ln2GM)

&#x20; 2\. Click the "Select Role" button in Step 1 to select a SHARD rarity.

![](https://lh5.googleusercontent.com/uklyxSrS8UydGC7CKG7IjoF7IoEj9Ltpw-MyN6PuYvpa7EYRL9b6L3BQnPW9ssysnPo862UaIb5RMv31DolvEkqV0puuylXyh\_Uk4BeeUkYVXn91XFQ2Mz3piTVa9dZd8Xo8GakNkH-GbpJhuU\_gEg8)

&#x20; 3\. The number of SHARD required for Card Fusion will also depend on the rarity. Click "Confirm" for the next step.

![](https://lh6.googleusercontent.com/Xb5vsb7wsJJzfnfW3gMyNluhGY79RWAy1cpwa6FVJ19imK9jwxoTBAvqM6130A1qCOLuV-Gy7T7yIky8TS5yWO9rJDnrKWY0Uuhc8FYehTs9H2qj-qzooxftcwt3VJl7ndp9zzeuaD3bRWZwQovzkSk)

&#x20; 4\. You can then see the amount of LUCID Basic and $XWG (BNB for a gas fee) you need to pay and the success rate of the Fusion mechanic. Click the "Start Fusion" button to begin the fusion.

![](https://lh4.googleusercontent.com/HKe3lrvq41MHeAH8yQ3riIttd9FKPPPdR7kOQgV\_mL0ri82Ik6Xf-SpEbLGKbsPAgjae0BggLdW22PPQNxXh5uRpiWuseXVkvE0EWEBRIp5s3Iv52kLFEVmOCfacE-PRwUEB76kdQ454Ox9-9REF-u0)

&#x20; 5\. The success rate of Card Fusion varies according to the rarity of the SHARD. The higher the rarity, the lower the success probability. A successful fusion will result in a new card of the same rarity. The attributes will be random.

![](https://lh4.googleusercontent.com/r0MNWcuYT6EOoe2YBV\_EZqk4u38qORhom1s4qPumvUe-r4TeH5C4Pim\_37xWJtY-QkqOKhtnajTpfS2Hy8oH\_RDGt5eF\_JxD3cLgoDR9QWXLeX1iTtw-Gjep8WJeRPn38d6UrBY9UBQ-RVDCTfMlSTs)

Congratulations! You have received a new Dream Card using our Fusion mechanism.
