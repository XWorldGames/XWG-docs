### **What is SHARD? How can I earn it?** <a href="#75e2" id="75e2"></a>

SHARDs will be the primary reward **** asset for <mark style="background-color:purple;">**LUCID Chests**</mark>, which are important for players who wish to use the Dream Card Fusion mechanism**.** SHARDs are **stackable** in a player's inventory.

There are 5 levels of rarity available for SHARDs, similar to the rarities for Dream Cards (Common, Rare, Epic, Legendary, Myth). \
\
The number of obtainable SHARDs from opening LUCID chests will be random but within a predetermined range.

### What Heroes will be available as SHARDs?

Not all Dream Card heroes will be available as Hero SHARDs, so be sure to keep an eye out for who is and isn’t available during each chest period.\
