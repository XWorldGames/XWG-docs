# Mystery Box

![Mystery Box](<../../.gitbook/assets/image (1).png>)

****[**Mystery Box**](https://nft.xwg.games/#/pages/index/mystery) **** also known as ‘blind box’, is a type of packaging that keeps its cards hidden. They are identical in every way, and nobody, including us, knows which unique character card is inside.
